Fatigue & Residual Life Assessment

Our Expertise

Detect Cracks Early, Prevent Failures, Prolong Asset Life

Aging steel bridges under heavy rail traffic accumulate fatigue from millions of load cycles. Our Fatigue & Residual Life Assessment uses detailed FEM and fracture-mechanics models to predict crack growth, estimate remaining service life, and deliver prioritized recommendations for strengthening, retrofitting, or replacement.

Overview

At Sentra, we help bridge owners and operators evaluate fatigue behavior and remaining service life under cyclic loads. Using advanced FEM, crack growth models, and field data, we deliver clear insights and actionable strategies—whether strengthening, retrofitting, or replacement—to reduce risk, extend service life, and optimize costs. Whether you need short-term construction monitoring or permanent asset surveillance, our solutions scale from single-structure deployments to city-wide sensor networks.
