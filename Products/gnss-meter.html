Overview

The Sentra GNSS Meter is a precision positioning sensor for long-term, continuous monitoring of bridges, tunnels, buildings, and slopes. It captures millimeter-level displacements using high-precision GNSS technology, with onboard algorithms to detect movement and deviations automatically. Data is transmitted to cloud or edge platforms in real time, eliminating the need for manual data collection and enabling immediate response to critical movements.
