Products Overview

The Sentra Edge Devices is engineered for continuous structural health monitoring across diverse environments. Whether deployed on bridges monitoring traffic-induced vibrations, buildings tracking seismic activity, or industrial equipment detecting mechanical wear, our sensor provides actionable insights that protect assets and lives.

Combining cutting-edge MEMS technology with long-range wireless communication, the sensor captures acceleration data across a wide frequency spectrum (0.1 Hz - 10 kHz) and transmits it securely to cloud-based platforms for real-time visualization and intelligent analysis.

Key Features & Specifications

Enterprise-grade hardware and software designed for mission-critical monitoring applications.

  • 3-axis MEMS accelerometer (±16g range)
  • Frequency range: 0.1 Hz - 10 kHz
  • LoRaWAN & 4G LTE connectivity
  • IP67 waterproof & dustproof rating
  • 5-10 year battery life (replaceable)
  • Real-time alerts & notifications
  • Cloud-based analytics platform
  • BIM/GIS integration ready
